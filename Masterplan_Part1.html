Pillar 1: Embodied AI

Pillar 2: Robot Vision

YOLOv8/v9, depth estimation, pose estimation, optical flow, and visual SLAM. Making robots see and understand the 3D world.

Pillar 3: Autonomous Navigation

ROS2, Nav2, SLAM Toolbox, sensor fusion (LiDAR + camera + IMU). Taking robots from point A to B safely and intelligently.

Pillar 5: Systems & MLOps

Docker, GitHub CI/CD, model quantization, edge inference on RPi 5, ROS2 lifecycle nodes, and real-time system design.
