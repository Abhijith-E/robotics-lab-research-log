Content Strategy

LinkedIn Growth Playbook

Don't just post — build a brand. Each post follows a proven high-engagement formula: Hook → Insight → Demo → Call-to-Action. Post on Sunday evening (7–9 PM IST = peak LinkedIn engagement in India).

Post Formula

📌 Anatomy of a Viral Robotics Post

  • Line 1 (Hook): Bold statement or surprising result. Under 12 words.
  • Lines 2–4: Explain what you built and why it matters.
  • Lines 5–8: Tech stack (shows depth). Keep it accessible.
  • Media: Always include a video demo or image collage — 5x more reach.
  • CTA: "GitHub link in comments" or "What would you build next?"
  • Hashtags: Max 5 targeted hashtags. No hashtag spam.

📈 Growth Tactics

  • Tag 2–3 relevant AI/robotics researchers or professors in each post (respectfully)
  • Cross-post to Twitter/X with a short clip — robotics community is large there
  • Post GitHub repo with every project — stars = social proof
  • Reply to every comment within 24 hours — boosts algorithm reach significantly
  • One "reflection" post per month: lessons learned, mistakes made — builds authenticity
  • Share arXiv preprint (Sprint 7) in ResearchGate + Academia.edu for academic visibility
